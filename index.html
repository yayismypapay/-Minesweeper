Goal

Open every safe cell without clicking on a mine. Mines stay hidden until you make a mistake or win.

02

Numbers

A number shows how many mines touch that cell: left, right, top, bottom, and diagonals.

03

Flags

If you think a closed cell has a mine, mark it with a flag. It helps you avoid dangerous cells.

04

Beginner strategy

  • Start by opening any cell. The first click is always safe in this version.
  • If a number is 1 and only one closed cell touches it, that closed cell is probably a mine.
  • If a number already has enough flags around it, the other nearby closed cells are safe.
  • Do not guess too fast. Compare several numbers near each other.
05

Controls

Computer: left click to open, right click to flag. Phone/tablet: use Flag mode, then tap cells to place or remove flags.
