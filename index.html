Goal

Your goal is to open all safe cells without clicking on a mine. Mines stay hidden until you make a mistake or win.

🔢

Numbers

A number shows how many mines touch that cell. Touching means around it: left, right, top, bottom, and diagonals.

Flags

If you think a cell has a mine, mark it with a flag. Flags help you remember dangerous cells.

🧠

Simple beginner strategy

  • Start by opening a random cell. The first click is always safe in this version.
  • If a cell shows 1 and only one closed cell touches it, that closed cell is probably a mine. Flag it.
  • If the number already has enough flags around it, the other nearby closed cells are safe.
  • Do not guess too fast. Look at several numbers together.
🕹️

Controls

Computer: left click to open, right click to flag. Phone/tablet: use Flag mode, then tap cells to place or remove flags.
